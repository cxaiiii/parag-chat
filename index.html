Meet Parag

Parag is a small chat model built by Chaitanya (cxaiiii), distilled from his own dataset. There is no server behind this page — the model itself downloads to your device and thinks right here in the browser.

Loads the newest Parag v4 (0.6B) — one-time download of ~380 MB, cached for next time. Inside, you can switch to the older v3 (0.5B) to compare. Wi-Fi and a laptop give the best speed.
